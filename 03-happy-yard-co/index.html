Common questions

Things folks ask.

What does the service include?

A thorough scooping of your yard, patio, or outdoor space — all visible waste, double-bagged and disposed of off-site. Weekly, bi-weekly, one-time. Whatever you need.

How much does it cost?

Weekly visits start at $19.99. Pricing scales gently with number of dogs and yard size. Use the booking flow above for a live price.

Do you service my area?

We cover Franklin, TN and most of Williamson County, plus southern Davidson. If you're nearby and not sure, drop a ZIP in the booking flow — we'll confirm before charging anything.

What about winter or bad weather?

We run year-round in light rain or snow. For severe weather we reschedule at no extra charge.

HOAs and apartments?

Yes — we offer bulk pricing and property-specific plans for HOAs, apartments, and commercial properties. Reach out for a custom quote.

How often should I schedule?

Most one- or two-dog households go weekly. Larger packs sometimes choose twice-weekly. Quieter yards do fine on bi-weekly.
