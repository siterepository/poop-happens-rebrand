01Editorial

The Sunday Yard

A magazine, not a service. Drop caps, italic decks, masthead rules.

  • Magazine framing. Issue number, "Dispatches" testimonials, editor's notes.
  • Warm-earth palette. Terracotta, sage, cream — never fluorescent.
  • Reserve-then-pay flow. 4 steps, half-rate first visit.

02Brutalist

Zero Tolerance

A logistics dashboard. Hazard yellow, monospaced specs, no slack.

  • Live quote calculator. Toggle inputs, see your number recalculate.
  • Operational tone. Route IDs, uptime, rain policy, gate-secure photos.
  • 4-step terminal booking. Card form, instant receipt.

03Cartoon

Happy Yard Co.

Stickers, blobs, confetti. The friendliest possible take.

  • 4-color saturated. Pink, sky, sun, grass. Wobble animations.
  • "Meet Ryan" polaroid. Badge stats, accordion FAQ.
  • Playful 4-step booking. Dog-name field for the photo log.

04Concierge

Estate Concierge

Navy, antique cream, brushed gold. Roman-numeraled memberships.

  • Interactive calendar booking. Date + hour, summary updates live.
  • Founder portrait. Sepia, signed note, "Seal of the House."
  • Higher-AOV positioning. Estates, HOAs, property managers.

Which One Wins

By scenario.

i.
For Google Ads / SEO

Zero Tolerance.

Live calculator + checkout in one scroll. Wins on comparison shoppers.

ii.
For Instagram / WOM

Happy Yard Co.

Sharable. Bright. Mascot-friendly. Wins on neighborhood Facebook.

iii.
For HOAs / Estates

Concierge.

Premium without smugness. The Leiper's Fork & property-manager play.

iv.
For brand / retention

Sunday Yard.

Slowest first conversion, strongest year-over-year LTV.
